Definition 14.

The Fourier Series of a function \(f\) defined on \((-p, p)\) is given by
\begin{align*} f(x) \amp = \frac{a_0}{2}+\sum_{n=1}^\infty\left(a_n\cos\frac{n\pi}{p}x+b_n\sin\frac{n\pi}{p}x\right),\\ a_0 \amp = \frac{1}{p}\int_{-p}^p f(x)dx\\ a_n \amp = \frac{1}{p}\int_{-p}^p f(x)\cos\frac{n\pi}{p}x dx \\ b_n \amp = \frac{1}{p}\int_{-p}^p f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi}{p}x dx \end{align*}
